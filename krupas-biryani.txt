- sweat safron in hot white wine or hot milk
- grind spices (cumin, coriander, tumeric, ?)
- mix spices with yogurt and/or ghee
- marinate chicken or lamb in the yogurt mix overnight
- soak basmati rice in water until a grain can barely crack, drain water
- fry chopped onions until brown
- in a large pan, layer with onion, then layer with rice, then layer with
  marinated chicken, then repeat
- pour the safron and liquid over the top
- cover and cook on the lowest heat possible for many hours
